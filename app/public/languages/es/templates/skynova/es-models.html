Materials

Steel & aluminum

Structural support for the dome. It can be combined with other materials.

Can be used on the shell to provide further waterproofing and sustainability for the rain.

Electrical speed controller

This is going to lower the speed of the drone when it gets nearby the hangars.

Power distribution board

Distributes power from the main power source to all components within the dome.
