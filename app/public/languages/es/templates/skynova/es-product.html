Components

Flight Management & Power Supply

Operators assign drones to calls, which then receive coordinates, execute tasks, and return to the hangar.
The station uses the main power grid and has emergency batteries powered by solar panels, providing 6-8 hours of backup.

Safety & Central Computer

Electrical safety features include surge protection and fire prevention mechanisms.
Central computer assists in drone assignment and tasks, with options for fiber optic connectivity.

Gates & Charging Modules

Aluminum roller gates are powered by electric motors, integrated with the central computer.
Computer Modules use QI wireless charging stations.

Space Organization & Security

Data and power are managed via Ethernet cables.
Drones are equipped with cameras, motion detection, and biometric authentication using Aldahua ASI6214S systems.
