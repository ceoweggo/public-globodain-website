About Our Product

Skynova is an innovative company transforming the way police, firefighters, and other emergency services respond to crises. Our mission is to provide instant access to drone hangars, significantly enhancing the efficiency and speed of emergency responses.

Partnership

Our Partnership


We integrate Flytbase's platform with our drone deployment infrastructure. We design, build, and maintain the hardware, while Flytbase manages the digital aspects.

Starlink

Starlink provides satellite internet access anywhere, ensuring high-speed connectivity even in remote locations.

FIWARE

FIWARE is an open-source platform for developing smart solutions for cities and IoT applications.

Communication equipment

A robust network with routers, switches, and cables is essential. Drones need high-quality video transmitters and a stable internet connection.

Energy for station

Continuous power is provided by a grid and UPS system. A Battery Management System ensures safe drone charging and performance.

Drone controlling

The Ground Control Station (GCS) allows operators to plan and monitor flights. Reliable flight controllers and GPS systems are crucial for navigation.

Servers and computers

The main server must be powerful and support various services such as flight control, communication, data handling, and security.

Components

Flight Management & Power Supply

Operators assign drones to calls, which then receive coordinates, execute tasks, and return to the hangar.
The station uses the main power grid and has emergency batteries powered by solar panels, providing 6-8 hours of backup.

Safety & Central Computer

Electrical safety features include surge protection and fire prevention mechanisms.
Central computer assists in drone assignment and tasks, with options for fiber optic connectivity.

Gates & Charging Modules

Aluminum roller gates are powered by electric motors, integrated with the central computer.
Computer Modules use QI wireless charging stations.

Space Organization & Security

Data and power are managed via Ethernet cables.
Drones are equipped with cameras, motion detection, and biometric authentication using Dahua ASI6214S systems.
